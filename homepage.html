Packs and Snacks Battling Hunger One Card at a Time

Who are we?

Packs and Snacks is a soon to be 501(c)(3) that aims to be a meta charity, an organization that aims to serve the charities around it. We host food eating contests with trading card booths with one purpose in mind — to raise money and awareness for Feeding America. We’ve held several events over the years, ranging from an oreo eating contest to a pizza eating contest and in total have raised 49,000 meals. For our future, we hope to provide many more nourishing fields as we aid local food and trading card businesses around the Glen Ellyn area.
